Build your Own Private ChatGPT

Build your own Bot/ChatGPT, PrivateGPT

ChatBot, ChatGPT, PrivateGPT start with similar, Common steps:

  • Determine what your building, will it be self contained? Will you reference any other informational data, Internet?:
  • Determine the appropriate Operating System, I typically choose Debian
  • Build your Hosting System 'minimalistic' to reduce surface exposure
  • Install all the AI Dependencies and Logging Capabilities
  • Download and install "AI: Program
  • Build, make, setup, download appropriate LLMS
  • Test Initial Chat Conversations with your ChatBot
  • Document, Document, Document



    • Using these steps, Your ChatBOT can now be expanded:

  • Assuming you havent tied your ChatBOT To the internet for references, continue on your path
  • 1. Ensure your System can not reach-out to the internet, block access
  • 2. Test Loading a falase document, such as a false baseball fact, and Test
  • 3. Establish Trust relationships and document them
  • 4. Ensure your system has RBAC, User account information
