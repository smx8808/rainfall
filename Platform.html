Distance education, also known as distance learning, is the education of students who may not always be physically present at school, or where the learner and the teacher are separated in both time and distance At the Cyber Learning Path, we want students to take a moment, and figure out what method is best for their learning This maybe in a classroom, in a virtual classroom or one-on-one, or even mentored based training. At Cyber Learning Path we understand every individual is an individual person and their learning methods maybe specific - This is why our platform "Virtual" offers many specifilzed virtual learning opportunties for students, from one-on-one to mentored guidance, to inclusive class-room learning.
